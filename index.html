Crystal Collector


  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game. You will need to obtain enough points to equal that number
  • There are 4 crystals on the bottom each with a different amount of points. The value is hidden until you click on a crystal.
  • You win the game once you match the right amount of points needed by clicking the crystals.
  • After winning the game will reset with new goal and different amount of points in each crystal.
